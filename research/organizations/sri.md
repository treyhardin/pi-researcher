# Stanford Research Institute (SRI)

Stanford Research Institute (SRI), now SRI International, is a research institute that focuses on the development of advanced scientific and technological solutions.

## Key Research Areas

* **Remote Viewing and Psychical Research:** Historically significant for its role in CIA-funded programs like SCANATE.
* **Computer Science and Networking:** Played a pivotal role in the development of the ARPANET and the early Internet.
* **Advanced Technologies:** Involvement in various high-tech research projects.
