# Organizations Research Summary

This directory contains research on various organizations, research facilities, and legislative components involved in the study, investigation, and management of aerial phenomena and related interests.

## Research Entries

### [AARO](aaro.md)
The All-domain Anomaly Resolution Office (AARO), the U.S. government's lead office for addressing UAP.

### [Advanced Aerospace Intelligence Integration](advanced-aerospace-intelligence-integration.md)
The intersection of military intelligence, specialized task forces (like AARO), and classified aerospace technology development in the study of UAP.

### [Condon Committee](condon-committee.md)
The University of Colorado UFO project (1966-1968) which investigated UFOs.

### [Denison University](denison-university.md)
Site of early Biefeld-Brown effect experiments.

### [The Disclosure Project](disclosure-project.md)
An organization founded by Dr. Steven Greer dedicated to the disclosure of UAP and advanced technology information.

### [Lockheed Corporation](lockheed-corporation.md)
Major aerospace and defense corporation.

### [Lockheed Martin](lockheed-martin.md)
Global security and aerospace company.

### [Martin Marietta](martin-marietta.md)
A major aerospace and defense corporation that merged with Lockheed in 1995.

### [NDAA Biological Evidence Provision](ndaa-biological-evidence.md)
Legislation within the National Defense Authorization Act relating to the investigation of non-human biologics.

### [NICAP](nicap.md)
The National Investigations Committee on Aerial Phenomena, a leading private UFO research organization.

### [Northrop Grumman](northrop-grumman.md)
Global aerospace and defense technology company.

### [Raytheon](raytheon.md)
Major defense contractor involved in radar and advanced electronic warfare.

### [Skunk Works](skunk-works.md)
The advanced development programs of Lockheed Martin.

### [Yeni Kent facility](yeni-kent-facility.md)
The facility in Turkey where the Kumburgaz UFO videos were recorded.

### [USS Princeton](uss-princeton.md)
Key role in detecting AAVs during the 2004 Nimitz UAP encounter.
