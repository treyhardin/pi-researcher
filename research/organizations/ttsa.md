# To The Stars Academy (TTSA)

To The Stars Academy of Science & Technology (TTSA) is a non-profit organization focused on the scientific study of Unidentified Anomalous Phenomena (UAP) and anomalous technologies.

## Focus Areas

* **UAP Disclosure:** Providing a platform for whistleblowers and researchers to share information about anomalous aerial phenomena.
* **Advanced Technologies:** Investigating potential technological breakthroughs associated with UAP.
* **Public Awareness:** Bringing scientific scrutiny and public attention to the phenomenon.
