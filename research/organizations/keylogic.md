# KeyLogic

## Overview
KeyLogic is a mid-tier company that provides data analytics, management services, and digital transformation solutions. It specializes in supporting critical missions within the energy, federal civilian, and defense sectors.

## Core Expertise & Services
KeyLogic offers a range of services designed to enhance operational efficiency and foster innovation:
- **Mission Management**
- **Systems Analysis**
- **Digital Transformation**
- **Cybersecurity**
- **Information Visualization**
- **Research and Development (R&D) Technology Management**

## Corporate Profile
- **Sectors Served:** Energy, Federal Civilian, Defense, and Commercial.
- **Headquarters:** Morgantown, West Virginia, USA.
- **Global Presence:** Canada, Sweden, and the United Kingdom.
- **Acquisition History:** Acquired by **System One** in 2023. It currently operates independently under **GAP Solutions**.
- **Company Size & Revenue:** An estimated 272 employees with an annual revenue of approximately $26.3M (based on available profiles).
