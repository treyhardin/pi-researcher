# Skinwalker Ranch

## Summary
Skinwalker Ranch, located in the Uinta Basin of Utah, is one of the most famous and active sites of anomalous phenomena in the world. It has been the subject of intense investigation by both private researchers and the US government, with reports of UAPs, poltergeist activity, cattle mutilations, and other unexplained events.

## Detailed Findings
The ranch has a long history of reported anomalous activity, much of which has been documented through various scientific and observational efforts.

### Historical Context
- **Native American Lore**: The area around the ranch is steeped in local legends and historical accounts of strange phenomena, some of which are linked to the Ute people of the region.
- **The Sherman Family**: The ranch was owned by the Sherman family for decades, during which time many of the most significant reports of anomalous activity occurred.

### Anomalous Phenomena
- **UAPs (Unidentified Anomalical Phenomena)**: Numerous sightings of Unidentified Aerial Phenomena have been documented over the ranch, often exhibiting extreme maneuverability and advanced propulsion characteristics.
- **Poltergeist Activity**: Frequent reports of unexplained physical objects moving or behaving erratically, and sudden, unexplained sounds.
- **Cattle Mutilations**: A pattern of livestock being found with precise, non-human surgical-like incisions, a phenomenon often linked to the ranch's activities.
- **Interdimensional/Otherworldly Presence**: Some researchers theorize that the ranch sits on a "portal" or intersection between dimensions, facilitating the appearance of entities or craft.

### Scientific Investigations
- **NIDS (National Institute for Discovery Science)**: Funded by Robert Bigelow, NIDS conducted significant research at the ranch, involving scientists like Hal Puthoff.
- **Skinwalker Ranch Experiments**: Various scientific instruments, including cameras, sensors, and even biological monitoring, have been deployed to capture data on the phenomena.

## Key Entities/Facts
- **Robert Bigelow**: Entrepreneur whose NIDS program was instrumental in investigating the ranch.
- **Hal Puthoff**: Scientist involved in the investigation of the ranch.
- **Uinta Basin, Utah**: The geographic location of the ranch.
- **Poltergeist Activity**: A recurring and significant phenomenon reported at the ranch.
