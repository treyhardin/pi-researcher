# The Hickson Tapes

## Summary
An audio recording of the distressed statements made by Charles Hickson and Calvin Parker following the 1973 Pascagula UFO incident.

## Detailed Findings
On October 11, 1973, Charles Hickson and Calvin Parker were fishing near the Pascagoula River in Mississippi. They encountered a silvery, approximately 100-foot-long craft that descended from above.

The men claim they were taken aboard the craft, examined by non-human entities, and then released.

The "Hickson Tapes" consist of the accounts given by the men to local authorities (Sheriff Fred Diamond and Detective Tom Huntley) shortly after the incident. The recordings capture their distressed and confused state.

The incident gained significant media attention, involving prominent UFO researchers like Dr. J Allen Hynek.

## Key Entities/Facts
- **Charles Hickson**: One of the two primary witnesses.
- **Calvin Parker**: The second primary witness.
- **Sheriff Fred Diamond**: The official who received the report.
- **Pascagoula, Mississippi**: The location of the encounter.
- **Date**: October 11, 1973.
