# Apollo Mission Sightings

## Summary
The Apollo program, NASA's series of lunar landing missions, has been the subject of numerous reports of Unidentified Aerial Phenomena (UAP) as seen by astronauts. While many sightings have been attributed to man-made debris or known celestial phenomena, recent declassified documents released in 2026 have brought renewed attention to specific encounters during the Apollo 12 and Apollo 17 missions.

## Detailed Findings

### Apollo 11
Reports involving the historic first lunar landing include:
- **Blue Orbs:** There are long-standing claims (often attributed to a highly debated theory by Otto Binder) that Commander Neil Armstrong witnessed two blue, pale-glowing orbs during the mission.
- **Buzz Aldrin's Observation:** Astronaut Buzz Aldrin has stated in interviews that he observed a light outside the window that appeared alongside the crew, though he has noted that his words are often taken out of context.

### Apollo 12
Lunar Horizon Light: Recent analysis of archival imagery from the Apollo 12 mission reveals an unidentified light source visible above the lunar horizon. This has been highlighted in recent science reporting as a significant piece of visual evidence from the lunar surface.

### Apollo 17 & Recent Pentagon Disclosures
- **2026 Pentagon Release:** In May 2026, the Pentagon released a collection of UAP/UFO sighting documents and media. Notably, some of these documents contain footage and data cataloged as being from the lunar surface during the Apollo 17 mission, suggesting potential encounters or observations that were previously classified.

### Historical Context and Debunking
- **Official Stance:** NASA officials, including Robert F. Allnut, have historically maintained that no definitive evidence of extraterrestrial spacecraft has been brought back from lunar or space voyages.
- **Alternative Explanations:** Many sightings, such as those during the Gemini missions, have been explained as man-made debris (e.g., Titan II second stages) or known space hardware (e.g., antenna covers).

## Key Entities/Facts
- **Astronauts:** Neil Armstrong, Buzz Aldrin.
- **Missions:** Apollo 11, Apollo 12, Apollo 17.
- **Key Event:** Pentagon UAP/UFO sighting documents release (May 2026).
- **Visual Evidence:** Declassified images showing unidentified lights on the lunar horizon.
