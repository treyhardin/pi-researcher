# UAP Disclosure Timeline

This document tracks the key milestones in the disclosure of Unidentified Anomalous Phenomena (UAP) to the public and government.

## Key Milestones

### Pre-2017: The Era of Secrecy
* (Historical context of investigations like Project Blue Book)

### 2017: The Turning Point
* **NY Times Article (December 2017):** The publication of the article regarding the existence of advanced aerial phenomena and the Pentagon's AATIP program.
* **Release of Declassified Videos:** The release of the "FLIR", "Gimbal", and "GoFast" videos.

### 2018 - Present: Increasing Transparency
* **UAP Task Force (UAPTF) Formation:** Establishment of the task force within the Department of Defense.
* **UAPTF Reports:** Regular updates and identified anomalies.
* **UAP Identification Program (UAPIP):** Transition from UAPTF to a more permanent program.
* **Congressional Hearings:** Various high-profile hearings featuring whistleblowers and intelligence officials.
