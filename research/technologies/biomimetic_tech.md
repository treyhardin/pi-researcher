# Biomimetic Technology

Biomimetic technology involves the design and production of materials, structures, and systems that are modeled on biological entities and processes.

## Connection to UAP

In the context of UAP research, biomimetic technology refers to the hypothesis that unidentified craft may utilize biological principles (such as morphogenesis and bioelectric signaling) for propulsion, structural adaptation, and self-repair.

## Key Areas of Study

* **Bio-hybrid Systems:** Integration of biological components with advanced engineering.
* **Morphogenetic Engineering:** Using developmental principles to create programmable matter.
* **Bio-electronic Propulsion:** Utilizing bio-electric signals for propulsion-related functions.
