# Propulsion Technologies Overview

## Summary
This document provides a high-level overview of the various anomalous and experimental propulsion technologies documented within this repository. The research focuses on phenomena that appear to defy conventional aerodynamics and Newtonian physics, often involving electromagnetic, plasma-based, or bio-electronic mechanisms.
