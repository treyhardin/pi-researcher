# Quantum Vacuum Physics

Quantum vacuum physics explores the properties of the vacuum state in quantum field theory, where the vacuum is not empty but filled with fluctuations of energy and particles.

## Theoretical Foundations

* **Zero-Point Energy:** The lowest possible energy that a quantum mechanical system may have.
* **Vacuum Fluctuations:** Temporary changes in the amount of energy in a point in space, as prescribed by the Heisenberg uncertainty principle.
* **Casimir Effect:** A physical force arising from quantized fields in the vacuum, often used to demonstrate the reality of vacuum fluctuations.

## Connection to UAP/NHI

* **Propulsion Mechanisms:** Hypothesized use of vacuum energy or manipulation of vacuum fluctuations for advanced propulsion (e.g., Alcubierre drive, electrogravitics).
* **Anomalous Energetics:** Investigation of whether UAP phenomena interact with or utilize the energy density of the quantum vacuum.
* **Spacetime Manipulation:** The theoretical possibility of manipulating the metric of spacetime through vacuum energy manipulation.
