# Morphogenetic Fields

Morphogenetic fields are hypothesized non-local, collective memory or information transfer fields that influence the development and form of organisms.

## Connection to Biology

The concept is closely linked to the process of **morphogenesis**, where biological development is shaped by concentration gradients and cellular processes.

## Potential Implications for UAP/NHI

* **Biological Engineering:** Possible use in the construction and self-repair of bio-hybrid craft.
* **Non-local Interaction:** The idea that unidentified phenomena could be influenced by information transfer across distances through these fields.
