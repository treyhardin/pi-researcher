# Annunaki

## Summary
The Anunnaki are a group of deities from ancient Mesopotamian mythology (Sumerian, Akkadian, Assyrian, and Babylonian). While historically documented as powerful gods tasked with decreeing human fate, they have become the centerpiece of modern "ancient astronaut" theories, most notably promoted by Zecharia Sitchin, who claimed they were extraterrestrial beings from a planet named Nibiru.

## Detailed Findings

### Historical and Mythological Context
The Anunnaki (also known as Anunaki, Anunna, or Ananaki) represent a high tier of the Mesopotamian pantheon. 
- **Origins:** According to various myths, they are the descendants of **An** (the sky god) and **Ki** (the earth goddess).
- **Role:** Their primary function was to oversee the destinies and fates of humanity.
- **Key Texts:**
    - *The Birth of the Great Gods and Goddesses*: Describes the separation of An and Ki, leading to the birth of the Anunnaki.
    - *Atrahasis Epic*: Details a labor dispute where the Anunnaki forced the **Igigi** (lesser deities) to carry the heavy workload of maintaining the earth, eventually leading to a rebellion.
    - *Enuma Elish*: The Babylonian creation myth involving the struggle against Tiamat, where the Anunnaki play a central role in the cosmic order.
- **Regional Variations:** Different city-states identified different numbers or groups of Anunaki, such as the "fifty Anunna of E/Eridu."

### The Ancient Astronaut Theory (Zecharia Sitchin)
In the late 20th century, author Zecharia Sitchin popularized a controversial interpretation of Sumerian texts.
- **The Nibiru Hypothesis:** Sitchin claimed the Anunnaki were advanced humanoid extraterrestrials from a planet called **Nibiru**, which follows a 3,600-year elliptical orbit around the Sun.
- **Gold Mining and Genetic Engineering:** He proposed that these beings traveled to Earth specifically to mine gold to repair their planet's atmosphere. To facilitate mining, they allegedly used genetic engineering to create humans by hybridizing their species with *Homo erectus*.
- **Legacy:** While extremely influential in modern UFO lore and conspiracy circles, these claims lack any supporting archaeological or biological evidence.

### Scientific and Archaeological Critiques
The "ancient astronaut" interpretation of the Anunnaki is widely rejected by the scientific community.
- **Linguistic Misinterpretation:** Archaeologists argue that Sitchin's theories rely on highly speculative and often incorrect translations of Sumerian and Akkadian cuneiform.
- **Lack of Physical Evidence:** There is no archaeological evidence (e.'g., advanced technology, extraterrestrial artifacts, or biological remains) to support the existence of Nibiru or the extraterrestrial origin of humans.
- **Archeological Consensus:** Mainstream scholars view the Anunnaki strictly as mythological figures within the established religious framework of ancient Mesopotamia.

## Key Entities/Facts
- **An (Anu):** Sky god, father of the Anunnaki.
- **Ki:** Earth goddess, mother of the Anunnaki.
- **Enlil:** Powerful deity, son of An and Ki, involved in the separation of heaven and earth.
- **Nibiru:** The hypothetical planet in Sitchin's theory.
- **Igigi:** The lesser deities who rebelled against the labor imposed by the Anunnaki.
- **Zecharia Sitchin:** The author who popularized the ancient astronaut theory of the Anunnaki.
