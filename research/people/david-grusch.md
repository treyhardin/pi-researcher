# David Grusch

## Summary
David Grusch (often referred to as David Charles Grusch) is a prominent U.S. whistleblower and former intelligence officer who, in 2023, ignited a massive wave of public and congressional interest in Unidentified Anomalous Phenomena (UAP). A former Major in the U.S. Air Force and a senior civilian official at the National Geospatial-Intelligence Agency (NGA), Grusch provided sworn testimony to the U.S. House Oversight and Accountability Committee, alleging that the United States government is conducting highly secretive, unauthorized programs involving the recovery and reverse-engineering of "non-human" spacecraft and biological remains.

## Detailed Findings
Grusch's disclosures are considered a watershed moment in the UAP disclosure movement due to his insider status and the gravity of his claims.

### Congressional Testimony (July 2023)
In July 2023, Grusch testified before the House Oversight and Accountability Subcommittee on Cybersecurity, Information Technology, and Government Innovation. His testimony included several explosive allegations:
- **Non-Human Biologics:** He testified under oath that the U.S. possesses "non-human biologics" recovered from "dozens" of crash-retrieval programs.
- **Secretive Spacecraft Programs:** He alleged the existence of much-secreted Special Access Programs (SAPs) dedicated to the recovery and reverse-engineering of extraterrestrial or non-human technology.
- **Lack of Oversight:** Grusch argued that these programs are operating without proper Congressional oversight, bypass the established intelligence community reporting structures, and are being hidden from elected officials and the public.
- **Threats and Retaliation:** He claimed that individuals attempting to report these programs have faced significant retaliation, including threats to their careers, security clearances, and even their lives.

### Intelligence and Military Career
Grusch's credentials lent significant weight to his allegations. He served for 14 years as an intelligence officer, including:
- **U.S. Air Force (USAF):** Served at the rank of Major.
- **National Geospatial-Intelligence Agency (NGA):** Served as a GS-15 civilian official (equivalent to a Colonel), where he was a co-lead for UAP and transmedium object analysis.
- **UAP Task Force (UAPTF) & AARO:** He was part of the intelligence community's effort to analyze UAP, reporting to the UAP Task Force and eventually the All-domain Anomaly Resolution Office (AARO).

### Impact on the Disclosure Movement
Grusch's testimony shifted the UAP conversation from "fringe" theories to a serious matter of national security, congressional oversight, and government transparency. His disclosures directly contributed to increased scrutiny of the Department of Defense's UAP-related activities and the push for the UAP Disclosure Act.

## Key Entities/Facts
- **Primary Role:** Whistleblower and former intelligence official.
- **Key Allegation:** Recovery of "non-human biologics" and spacecraft.
- **Core Issues:** Lack of Congressional oversight, illegal Special Access Programs (SAPs), and government suppression of UAP information.
- **Related Organizations:** National Geospatial-Intelligence Agency (NGA), National Reconnaissance Office (NRO), U.S. House Committee on Oversight and Accountability, AARO.
