# Leslie Kean

## Summary
American investigative journalist and author who played a pivotal role in bringing UAP (Unidentified Aerial Phenomena) into the mainstream public and governmental discourse. Her investigative work, particularly her book *UFOs: Generals, Pilots, and Government Officials Go on the Record*, has been instrumental in the recent wave of UAP disclosure.

## Detailed Findings
*   **Background:** An investigative journalist with a background in human rights (Burmese democracy). She transitioned to UAP research after receiving a significant report from a French colleague in 1999.
*   **Key Work - *UFOs* (2016):** This book presented documented accounts from high-ranking military and government officials, challenging the stigma surrounding the subject and providing a basis for more serious investigation.
*   **Impact on Disclosure:** Her reporting is credited with helping force the U.S. government (including the Pentagon) to acknowledge and investigate UAPs more formally.
*   **Other Work:** Also an author of *Surviving Death*, which explores evidence for an afterlife.

## Key Entities/Facts
*   **Ralph Blumenthal:** Co-reporter on major UAP investigations.
*   **David Grusch:** U.S. intelligence official and whistleblower whose claims were reported by Kean and Blumenthal.
*   **1999 French Report:** A 90-page report on UFO sightings by pilots that served as a catalyst for her involvement.
*   **Major Publications:** *The New Yorker*, *The<em>Guardian</em>*, *Columbia Journalism Review*.
