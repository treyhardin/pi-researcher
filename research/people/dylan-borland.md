# Dylan Borland

## Summary
Dylan Borland is a former U.S. Air Force (USAF) geospatial intelligence specialist and a prominent federal whistleblower. He gained national attention following his September 2025 testimony before the House of Representatives Task Force regarding his encounter with Unidentified Aerial Phenomena (UAP) and the subsequent retaliation he faced from government agencies.

## Detailed Findings
Borland's testimony highlights the critical issue of whistleblower protection and the potential existence of suppressed information regarding advanced aerospace technologies.

### The 2012 Encounter
- **Location:** Langley Air Force Base, Virginia.
- **The Phenomenon:** Borland witnessed a massive, approximately 100-foot, equilateral triangle-shaped craft.
- **Characteristics:** He described the craft as silent and noted that its material appeared "fluid" or "dynamic." Crucially, he reported that the craft interfered with his telephone during the encounter.
- **Timeline:** The event occurred while he was serving in an active-duty enlisted capacity (part of his service from 2010 to 2013).

### Whistleblowing and Retaliation
- **Nature of Disclosure:** Borland came forward as a whistleblower regarding both the specific UAP encounter and his knowledge of "legacy UAP programs" within the military and intelligence community.
- **Alleged Retaliation:** He testified that his career was effectively destroyed by sustained retaliation from government agencies. This included:
    - Professional obstruction and workplace harassment.
    - Medical malpractice.
 $\rightarrow$ Many whistleblowers face similar patterns of reprisal, which Borland highlighted as a major barrier to transparency.
- **Impact:** Borland has stated that reporting these incidents has left him without foreseeable professional prospects, emphasizing the high personal cost of UAP disclosure.

### Professional Background
- **Specialization:** "1N1" geospatial intelligence specialist.
- **Expertise:** Experience in analyzing video, radar, and advanced electro-optical imagery for official identification of aerial order of battle, as well as naval and ground targets.
- **Post-Military Career:** Worked for industry leaders such as BAE Systems and Intrepid Solutions as a Senior Analyst.

## Key Entities/Facts
- **Langley Air Force Base:** The site of the primary UAP encounter.
- **House of Representatives Task Force:** The congressional body to which he provided testimony in September 2025.
- **Legacy UAP Programs:** The secret programs Borland alleges are being shielded from public and congressional oversight.
- **Retaliation/Reprisal:** The central theme of his struggle within the intelligence and military communities.
