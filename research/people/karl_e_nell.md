---# Karl E. Nell

## Overview
Colonel (Ret.) Karl E. Nell is a prominent figure in the discourse surrounding Unidentified Anomalous Phenomena (UAP) and Non-Human Intelligence (NHI). A retired U.S. Army Intelligence Officer and decorated veteran, Nell has leveraged his deep connections within the Pentagon and the aerospace industry to advocate for transparency and government disclosure regarding UAP.

## Military and Professional Background
- **Military Service:** Retired U.S. Army Colonel. 
- **Pentagon Involvement:** Served as the Army Director supporting the Pentagon's **Unidentified Anomally Phenomena Task Force (UAPTF)** from 2021 to 2022.
- **Intelligence Expertise:** His career as an intelligence officer provides him with unique insights into the classified nature of UAP-related programs and the complexities of government secrecy.
- **Aerospace & Technology Leadership:** Possesses over 30 years of experience in organizational and technology leadership at major firms, including:
    - Bell Telephone Laboratories
    - Lockheed Martin Missiles & Space
    - TASC
    - CACI
    - ENSCO
- **Current Ventures:** Involved in the development of advanced technologies (e.g., meta-materials generation) through startups.

## Contributions to UAP Disclosure & NHI Discourse
- **Public Advocacy:** Nell has become a "credible insider" voice, moving from the shadows of intelligence to public forums (such as the SALT iConnections conference) to discuss the reality of UAP.
- **Corroboration of Whistleblower Claims:** He is notable for publicly corroborating the claims made by whistleblower **David Grusch** regarding the existence of terrestrial programs aimed at reverse-engineering technologies of non-human origin.
- **Key Assertions:**
    - **Existence of NHI:** He has stated publicly that "nonhuman intelligence exists" and has been interacting with humanity.
    - **Ongoing Interaction:** He asserts that this interaction is not a new phenomenon but has been ongoing.
    - **Call for Democratic Oversight:** He advocates for bringing UAP secrecy under democratic control, arguing that certain "unelected people in the government" are aware of these phenomena but are withholding information from the public and elected officials.
- **Notable Presentations/Publications:**
    - **SALT iConnections (2024):** Presented on the existence of NHI.
    - **The Sol Foundation:** Contributed to "The UFO Enigma: A Call for US Government Disclosure" (May 2025).
    - **YouTube/Public Lectures:** Participated in public discussions (e.g., with Alex Klokus) regarding the history and future of UAP disclosure.

## Key Themes in His Work
- **Transparency vs. Secrecy:** The tension between national security interests and the public's right to know about NHI.
- **Reverse Engineering:** The pursuit and potential existence of programs focused on studying crashed or recovered UAP technology.
- **Institutional Integrity:** The need to reform the way the US government handles and reports on anomalous phenomena.

## Related Figures and Entities
- **David Grusch:** Whistleblower whose claims Nell has supported.
- **UAP Task Force (UAPTF) / AARO:** The primary government entities Nell's work centers around.
- **The Sol Foundation:** An organization Nell has collaborated with for publications.
