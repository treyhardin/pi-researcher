# Luis Elizondo

## Summary
Luis Elizondo (often known as "Lue" Elizondo) is a prominent American author, media personality, and UAP whistleblower. A former U.S. Army counterintelligence officer, he is widely recognized for his role in bringing public attention to Unidentified Anomalous Phenomena (UAP) and his involvement in various Department of Defense (DoD) programs related to UAP investigation.

## Detailed Findings
Elizondo's career is deeply intertwined with the U.S. government's UAP disclosure efforts. 

### Career and Role in UAP Programs
- **Department of Defense (DoD) Background:** He previously served in the U.S. Army's counterintelligence unit and worked within the DoD.
- **AATIP and AAWSAP:** He played a significant role in the Advanced Aerospace Threat Identification Program (AATIP) and the Advanced Aerospace Weapon System Applications Program (AAWSAP), programs designed to study UAP phenomena.
- **Whistleblower Status:** He became a prominent whistleblower, alleging that excessive secrecy, "stove-piping," and even active suppression and intimidation campaigns were used by a "cabal" within the government to hide the existence of UAP and related technologies.
- **Congressional Testimony:** In November 2024, he provided highly publicized testimony before the U.S. House Committee on Oversight and Accountability, where he testified that UAP are real and that advanced technologies—not made by the US government—are monitoring sensitive military installations globally. He also alleged that the U.S. possesses UAP technologies.

### Key Allegations and Claims
- **Suppression and Intimidation:** Elizondo claimed to have been personally targeted by unwarranted criminal investigations, harassment, and efforts to destroy his credibility by individuals within the government.
- **Secretive Arms Race:** He warned of a multi-decade, secretive arms race funded by misallocated taxpayer dollars, involving the development and monitoring of UAP technologies.
- **Governmental Failure:** He criticized the lack of coordination between agencies like the CIA, NASA, the Pentagon, and the Department of Energy, advocating for a single, whole-of-government point of contact for UAP issues.

## Key Entities/Facts
- **Official Title/Role:** Former U.S. Army Counterintelligence Officer; Former manager of a Special Access Program (SAP) on behalf of the White House and the National Security Council.
- **Primary Focus:** UAP disclosure, transparency, and investigating government-led suppression of UAP information.
- **Major Event:** Testimony before the U.S. $118^{th}$ Congress (November 2024).
