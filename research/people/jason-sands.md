# Jason Sands

## Summary
Jason Sands is a retired U.S. Air Force Master Sergeant and whistleblower who has come forward with claims regarding his involvement in clandestine UAP/NHI programs and personal encounters with non-human intelligence.

## Detailed Findings
* **Military Background:** Sands served over 21 years in the U.S. Air Force, initially as a B-52 crew chief before transitioning into intelligence work.
* **UAP Involvement:** He claims to have been part of a "legacy UAP program" (often referred to as "The Program") and has held roles that allowed him access to sensitive information regarding black budget operations.
* **Encounter Claims:** Sands has detailed a traumatic experience at a UFO crash site where he observed a "blue" non-human entity. He has also mentioned hearing discussions regarding "Tall Whites" and "Greys."
* **Public Advocacy:** He established the **Firsthand Fund**, an initiative intended to assist UAP witnesses in sharing their firsthand accounts and protecting their anonymity where necessary.
* **Media Presence:** His disclosures have been featured in significant media outlets, including James Fox's documentary *The Program* and the *Joe Rogan Experience* (Episode #2264).

## Key Entities/Facts
* **Affiliations:** U.S. Air Force (Retired), The Firsthand Fund.
* **Related Personnel:** [David Grusch](david-grusch.md) (Sands has reportedly discussed his experiences with Grusch).
* **Key Events:** Alleged encounter at a crash site involving a "blue" entity; involvement in a 20-year black budget program.
