# Jake Barber

## Overview
Jake Barber is a U.S. Air Force veteran and former helicopter pilot who has emerged as a whistleblower regarding Unidentified Aerial Phenomena (UAP) and government retrieval programs.

## Key Details
- **Background:** U.S. Ast Air Force veteran, former helicopter pilot, and founder of "Skywatcher."
- **Role in UAP Claims:** Claims firsthand involvement in missions related to the retrieval of non-human technology.
- **Major Allegations:**
    - **Retrieval Operations:** Alleges that the U.S. government has been involved in recovering and studying downed craft of non-human origin.
    - **Specific Craft:** Recounted an operation involving the retrieval of a peculiar, white, egg-shaped craft.
    - **Psionic Experiments:** Has made claims regarding psionic experiments and their connection to UAP research.
    - **Health Impacts:** Has reported health struggles/crises that he links to exposure during his missions.
- **Prominent Interviews:** Has appeared on [[people/ross-coulthart]]'s programs (e.g., *Reality Check* on NewsNation) to share his testimony.
- **Evidence:** Claims to possess documentation of his service and other evidence that he intends to release to support his claims.
