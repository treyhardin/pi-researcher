# Murat Yalcin Yalman

## Summary
Murat Yalcin Yalman is a Turkish night security guard who documented a series of significant unidentified aerial phenomena (UAP) over the Sea of Marmara near Kumburgaz, Turkey, between 2007 and 2009. Using a telephoto lens, he captured over 25 videos of metallic, disc-shaped, and oval-shaped objects.

## Detailed Findings
Murat Yalcin Yalman worked the night shift near the Yeni Kent facility in the coastal town of Kumburgaz. Over several years (2007–2009), he recorded numerous videos of objects appearing over the water. The objects often appeared as metallic, disc-like, or oval-shaped crafts. Some sightings were accompanied by red and orange lights. The footage has been analyzed by various UFO researchers and organizations, highlighting its significance due to the volume and nature of the captures.

## Key Entities/Facts
- **Role:** Night security guard at the Yeni Kent facility.
- **Location:** Kumburgast, Turkey (Sea of Marmara).
- **Timeframe:** 2007–2009.
- **Nature of Objects:** Metallic, disc-shaped, or oval-shaped; occasionally accompanied by red/orange lights.
- **Quantity of Footage:** Over 25 separate videos.
