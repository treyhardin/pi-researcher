# Danny Sheehan

## Summary
American constitutional attorney, public interest lawyer, and educator who is a leading figure in the movement for UAP (Unidentified Anomalous Phenomena) disclosure. As the President of the New Paradigm Institute, he represents whistleblowers and advocates for legislative action to compel government transparency regarding UAP programs.

## Detailed Findings
*   **Legal Background:** A highly accomplished constitutional litigator with a career spanning five decades. His work includes representing parties in high-profile cases such as the Pentagon Papers, Watergate, and Iran- Contra.
*   **UAP Advocacy:**
    *   **New Paradigm Institute (NPI):** Founded NPI as an initiative of the Romero Institute to advocate for the full and responsible disclosure of UAPs from governments and corporations.
    *   **Whistleblower Representation:** He represents various whistleblowers, most notably **David Grusch**, who testified before Congress about alleged secret UAP crash retrieval and reverse-engineering programs.
    *   **Legislative Efforts:** He has been heavily involved in advocating for the **UAP Disclosure Act of 2023** and the inclusion of UAP-related language in the **National Defense Authorization Act (NDAA)**. He has been a vocal critic of loopholes and ambiguous language in such legislation.
    *   **Criticism of Official Denials:** He has openly challenged officials like Dr. Sean Kirkpatrick (former AARO director) regarding the lack of transparency and the denial of evidence regarding UAP programs.
*   **Educational Background:** Harvard-trained (BA, JD, and Divinity School).

## Key Entities/Facts
*   **David Grusch:** Major whistleblower represented by Sheehan.
*   **New Paradigm Institute (NPI):** The organization Sheehan leads for UAP disclosure.
*   **Romero Institute:** The public-interest organization Sheehan has led for over 30 years.
*   **UAP Disclosure Act of 2023:** Key legislation he has advocated for.
*   **National Defense Authorization Act (NDAA):** The major defense bill where he has fought for UAP language.
