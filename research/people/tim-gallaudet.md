# Dr. Tim Gallaudet

## Summary
Dr. Tim Gallault is a Retired U.S. Navy Rear Admiral and former official at the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (NOAA) who has become a prominent figure in the movement for UAP (Unidentified Aerial Phenomena) transparency. In November 2024, he provided critical testimony to Congress, acting as a whistleblower to expose alleged government non-disclosure and disinformation campaigns regarding UAP phenomena.

## Detailed Findings
Dr. Gallaudet's testimony, primarily delivered before the House Oversight Committee, focuses on the lack of transparency and the active suppression of information regarding UAPs within the U.S. government.

### Key Points of Testimony:
- **Call for Oversight:** Gallaudet strongly recommended the establishment of rigorous oversight for the Executive Branch's management of UAP programs, materials, and information. He highlighted the need to investigate the legitimacy of the information being provided by agencies like AARO (All-domain Anomaly Resolution Office).
- **Addressing AARO Inaccuracies:** He specifically pointed to the need for investigating alleged inaccuracies in AARO's historical records reports, suggesting that the agency's reporting may not reflect the full reality of UAP encounters.
- **Whistleblower Support:** A central theme of his testimony was the provision of "top cover" for individuals—both military and civilian—who have come/come forward to disclose UAP-related information. He highlighted the necessity of protecting these individuals from retaliation.
- **Allegations of Disinformation:** Gallaudet alleged that certain elements within the government are actively engaged in a disinformation campaign designed to discredit UAP whistleblowers through personal attacks and the manipulation of information.
- **Moral Leadership:** He emphasized the importance of moral leadership in handling the UAP issue, urging the nation's leaders to embrace transparency rather than continuing a policy of non-disclosure.

## Key Entities/Facts
- **Dr. Tim Gallaudet:** Retired U.S. Navy Rear Admiral; former NOAA official; whistleblower/witness.
- **[[organizations/aaro|AARO (All-domain Anomaly Resolution Office)]]:** The government office responsible for investigating UAPs, which Gallaudet identified as needing better oversight.
- **Congressional Hearings (Nov 2024):** The platform through which Gallaudet provided his critical testimony.
- **Whistleblower Protections:** A key area of concern raised by Gallaudet regarding the safety and credibility of those disclosing UAP information.
