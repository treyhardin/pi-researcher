# Dr. James T. Lacatski


## Summary
Dr. James T. Lacatski is a retired Defense Intelligence Agency (DIA) engineer, analyst, and nuclear engineer by training. He is most recognized for his role as the Program Manager of the Advanced Aerospace Weapon Systems Applications Program (AAWSAP), which was one of the largest and most ambitious U.S. government-funded investigations into Unidentified Aerial Phenomena (UAP) and anomalous aerospace technologies. Through his work and subsequent books, he has brought significant visibility to the existence of secret government investigations and the potential possession of advanced technology of unknown origin.

## Detailed Findings
Dr. Lacatski's career and disclosures have fundamentally altered the landscape of UAP research from simple "lights in the sky" sightings to a serious investigation into advanced physics, propulsion, and the potential for extraterrestrial or non-human technological presence.

### AAWSAP and Government Investigation
- **Role in AAWSAP:** As the program manager for AAWSAP, Lacatski oversaw a $22 million effort focused on researching the physical effects, propulsion mechanisms, and biological implications of UAP encounters.
- **Scope of Research:** The program sought to understand the engineering and paranormal phenomena associated with anomalous objects, including investigations into "Skinwalker Ranch" and related aerospace threats.
- **Contracting:** The program involved significant contracting with entities such as Bigelow Aerospace.

### Key Disclosures and Claims
- **Advanced Technology/Craft:** In various interviews and writings, Lacatski has claimed that the United States government possesses craft of unknown origin and has access to their interiors, suggesting a level of technological sophistication far beyond publicly known capabilities.
- **Whistleblower and Author:** He has authored several books (e.g., *Inside the U.S. Government's Covert UFO Program*, *Skinwalkers at the Pentagon*) that reveal details of the government's secret UAP/UFO programs and the intense interest in anomalous aerospace technologies.
- **The "Immaculate Constellation" Connection:** His work and the programs he managed are often cited in investigations into more recent programs like "Immaculate Constellation," which allegedly emerged after the New York Times' 2017 disclosures.

### Professional Background
- **Expertise:** A nuclear engineer with expertise in fusion plasmas and directed energy.
- **Intelligence Career:** Served as a career intelligence analyst and engineer within the Defense Intelligence Agency (DIA).

## Key Entities/Facts
- **AAWSAP (Advanced Aerospace Weapon Systems Applications Program):** The primary government-funded investigation program managed by Lacatski.
- **Defense Intelligence Agency (DIA):** The intelligence agency under which Lacatski performed his research.
- **Skinwalker Ranch:** A notable location associated with the types of phenomena investigated under his management.
- **Nuclear Engineering:** His technical foundation which informed his approach to investigating advanced propulsion and energy.
