---# Senator Mike Rounds

## Summary
U.S. Senator Mike Rounds (R-SD) is a key legislative figure in the movement for transparency regarding Unidentified Anomalologous Phenomena (UAP). He has been a leading advocate for legislative efforts to mandate the disclosure of UAP-related information to the public and Congress.

## Legislative Efforts
Senator Rounds has been instrumental in championing bipartisan legislation, notably the **UAP Disclosure Act**, which seeks to establish procedures for the declassification of sensitive information related to unidentified phenomena. His work focuses on ensuring that the intelligence community provides transparent reports to both the Congressional intelligence committees and the public.

## Congressional Role
A member of both the **Senate Armed Services Committee** and the **Senate Intelligence Committee**, Rounds occupies a unique position that allows him to oversee the policies and programs of the Department of Defense and the intelligence community. This position has enabled him to push for increased accountability and transparency in how UAP-related data is handled and shared.

## Key Objectives
*   **Transparency:** Promoting the declassification of information pertaining to UAP sightings and investigations.
*   **Accountability:** Ensuring that the executive branch and intelligence agencies are held accountable for their handling of UAP-related data.
*   **Bipartisan Cooperation:** Working with colleagues on both sides of the aisle to advance legislative frameworks for disclosure.
