# Proposed Maps of Content (MOCs)

This document proposes the initial structure for primary knowledge hubs within the phenomenon research project. The goal is to transition from a folder-based hierarchy to a networked MOC/Wikilink pattern.

## 1. Primary Knowledge Hubs (Thematic)

These are high-level MOCs that aggregate content across multiple sub-directories based on subject matter rather than just file location.

### 🧬 The Biological Frontier
*Aggregates research related to life forms, biology, and the interaction between technology and biology.*
- **Core Areas:** `research/biology`, `research/technologies/synthetic-biology`, `research/technologies/xenobot`, `research/technologies/bio-electronic-propulsion`.
- **Key Links:** Plasma-based life, Xenobiology, Biological interface technologies.

### 🛸 Phenomenological Encounters (The Case Studies Hub)
*Aggregates all documented sightings, encounters, and historical incidents.*
- **Core Areas:** `research/cases`, `research/public/notable-figures-overview`.
- **Key Links:** Nimitz encounter, Roswell, WWII Foo Fighters, Transmedium phenomena.

### ⚙️ Advanced Technology & Propulsion
*The central hub for the "how" of the phenomenon.*
- **Core Areas:** `research/technologies`, `research/physics`, `research/protocols`.
- **Key Links:** Electrogravitics, Biefeld-Brown effect, Alcubierre drive, Plasma propulsion, Metamaterials.

### 🏛️ Governance, Policy & Organizations
*Focuses on the human structures involved in research, disclosure, and containment.*
- **Core Areas:** `research/government-and-policy`, `research/organizations`, `research/people`.
- **Key Links:** AARO, CIA, Lockheed Martin, Disclosure timeline, Legislative efforts.

### 🧠 Theoretical Frameworks & Physics
*The intellectual models used to explain the phenomenon.*
- **Core Areas:** `research/theories`, `research/physics`, `research/mythology` (as cultural theory).
- **Key Links:** Interdimensional hypothesis, Fermi Paradox, Spacetime metric engineering, Morphogenetic fields.

### 📜 The Historical & Mythological Context
*Explores the long-term human relationship with anomalous phenomena.*
- **Core Areas:** `research/mythology`, `research/books`.
- **Key Links:** Anunnaki, Book of Enoch, Ancient Astronaut theory.

## 2. Implementation Strategy

1.  **Baseline Creation:** Create `.md` files for each MOC listed above in the `research/` root or designated `mocs/` folder.
2.  **Content Population:** Populate each MOC with links to existing documents.
3.  **Inter-linkage Mapping:** Ensure each MOC points to at least two other relevant MOCs (e. hyperlinking the hubs themselves).
4.  **Directory Cleanup:** As MOCs mature, evaluate if sub-directories can be further consolidated or reorganized to reflect these thematic groupings.
