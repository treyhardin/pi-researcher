# Summary of Mythology/Entities Research

## Igigi
Research into the Igili reveals them as a group of celestial deities in Mesopotamian mythology, known for their role as subordinate gods who eventually rebelled against the labor imposed by the Anunnaki, leading to the creation of humanity.

## The Hangar 18 Legend
An exploration of the conspiracy theory regarding a secret facility at Wright-Patterson Air Force Base allegedly housing extraterrestrial debris and biological remains, popularized by Robert Spencer Carr.
