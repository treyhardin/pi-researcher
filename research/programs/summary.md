# 📋 Historical UAP Programs (MOC)

This Map of Content (MOC) organizes research into various intelligence and military programs tasked with investigating or managing Unidentified Anomalous Phenomena.

## 🔍 Investigation & Disclosure Programs
Programs primarily focused on the identification, investigation, and public/internal reporting of UAP.
* [[research/project-blue-book|**Project Blue Book**]] - The foundational US Air Force investigation into UFOs.
* [[research/project-sign|**Project Sign**]] - Early US Air Force study of unidentified aerial phenomena.
* [[research/aatip|**AATIP**]] - Advanced Aerospace Threat Identification Program.

## 🧠 Intelligence & Psychological Research
Programs involving the intersection of human intelligence, psychological operations, and anomalous data.
* [[research/mk-ultra|**MK-Ultra**]] - Historical research into behavioral modification and its intersections with anomalous findings.
