# Programs Summary

## Intelligence and UAP Programs
- [Project MKUltra](../programs/mk-ultra.md): A CIA-led human experimentation program focused on behavioral modification and mind control.
- [AATIP](../programs/aatip.md): The Advanced Aerospace Threat Identification Program, a DoD program studying UAP.
- [Project Blue Book](../programs/project-blue-book.md): The USAF study of UFOs from 1952 to 1969.
- [Project Sign](../programs/project-sign.md): An early USAF investigation into UFOs.
