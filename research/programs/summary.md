# Programs Research Summary

This directory contains research reports on various government and private programs related to UAP and aerospace investigations.

## Research Entries

### [AATIP](../programs/aatip.md)
The Advanced Aerospace Threat Identification Program, a DoD-funded initiative that investigated UAP/UFOs and contributed to modern disclosure.

### [Project Blue Book](../programs/project-blue-book.md)
The official U.S. Air Force investigation into UFOs from 1952 to 1969.

### [Project Sign](../programs/project-sign.md)
An early Air Force investigation into UFOs, precursor to Project Grudge and Project Blue Book.
